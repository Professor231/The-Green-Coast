Land Info

Land conflict over the years has been a serious issue and bone of contention amongst numerous people across the country. This problem still exists today as a result of poor land management, little to no access to adequate land information, and absence of an effective system to track land ownership across the country. Hence, At Green Coast Engineering, we have developed a solution to solve this age old problem through the use of GIS.
