Principal

Green Coast Engineering & Geo Consultants is a Liberian owned and operated service-based entity that Collects, Processes, and Publishes Geo-data (information) that influences adequate Land and Resource Management and decision-making across social and environmental services. Our products include Geographic Surveys, Land Data Management, Data Analysis and Visualization (web map/operational dashboard), and Geo Consultancy.

At Green Coast, we offer a range of products and services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients: Geographic Surveys: Our experienced team conducts comprehensive geographic surveys using state-of-the-art equipment and techniques. From land topography to environmental assessments, we gather accurate data to support informed decision-making.

Land Data Management: We specialize in organizing and managing land-related data, including parcel information, land use classifications, and property boundaries. Our expertise in data management ensures that clients have access to reliable and up-to-date information for planning and development purposes.

Data Analysis and Visualization: Using advanced tools and technologies, we analyze geospatial data to extract valuable insights and trends. We create interactive web maps and operational dashboards that allow users to visualize complex data sets and make data-driven decisions.

Geo Consultancy: Our team of skilled consultants provides expert advice and guidance on a wide range of geospatial issues. Whether it's developing land management strategies or addressing environmental challenges, we offer tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each client.

At Green Coast, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and value to our clients. By harnessing the power of geospatial data, we help organizations make informed decisions that drive positive social, economic, and environmental outcomes.
